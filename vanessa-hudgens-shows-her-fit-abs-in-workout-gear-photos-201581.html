Vanessa Hudgens Shows Her Fit Abs in Workout Gear: Photos

Lethal weapons!  

Vanessa Hudgens displayed her dangerously chiseled abs while out on a gym run in Los Angeles on Christmas Eve.

It seems the actress, 26, wanted to squeeze in one more workout before heading into the holidays — and prior to her big debut in the Broadway musical Gigi, which runs in New York City from January 16 to February 12.
