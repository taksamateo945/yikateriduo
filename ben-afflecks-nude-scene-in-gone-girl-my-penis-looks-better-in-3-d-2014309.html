Ben Affleck's Nude Scene in Gone Girl: My Penis &quot;Looks Better in 3-D!&quot;

Whoa there! Ben Affleck got candid while speaking with MTV News about going full frontal in Gone Girl. The 42-year-old Oscar winner joked during an interview alongside costar Rosamund Pike that his penis “looks better in 3-D!”

“I think there’s some brief nudity in this movie,” Affleck told MTV News‘ Josh Horowitz when asked if viewers will be getting a peek. “Director David [Fincher] said to me from the beginning, ‘This is a warts and all movie. It can have no vanity. You have to see the naked underbelly of this character. It’s gotta be like in Europe, they just don’t give a s–t about any of that stuff and they just play real people’…[So] there is some brief, very brief nudity, I think,” he added.

“We’re selling this on the penis!” Horowitz then quipped, leading Jennifer Garner‘s hubby to exclaim: “The penis is in there!”
