Who Is Alexee Trevizo?

In a shocking turn of events, a 19-year-old woman named Alexee Trevizo has been charged with first-degree murder for allegedly concealing her deceased newborn in a trash can at Artesia General Hospital’s emergency room.

The investigation into this distressing incident began on January 27.

Trevizo, who was detained on May 10 before her graduation, was later released after posting a $100,000 bond.

Recently, the police made public the body camera footage in which Trevizo can be seen confessing to her crime in the presence of her mother.

The video serves as a crucial piece of evidence in the case. It is reported that in December 2022, Alexee Trevizo sought medical assistance at a hospital in Artesia, New Mexico, complaining of significant back pain.

She received treatment and was subsequently discharged.

Following her initial visit in December, Alexee Trevizo returned to the hospital a few weeks later in January, this time complaining of back discomfort.

According to medical staff, she proceeded to lock herself in a bathroom where she gave birth to a baby. Shockingly, Trevizo then placed the newborn inside a trash can before leaving the premises.

The Artesia Police Department recently charged Trevizo with first-degree murder, as well as child abuse resulting in death and tampering with evidence.

These charges reflect the gravity of the situation.

The body camera footage captured the moment when the police confronted the 19-year-old, revealing a startling confession.

After the incident, the body of the newborn was taken to the Office of the Medical Investigator of New Mexico for an autopsy.
