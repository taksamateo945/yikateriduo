Model Georgia Fowler is a multitasking mother as she pumps breast milk for her baby Dylan at work

Model Georgia Fowler has been working on modelling campaigns at her home in Sydney after giving birth to her daughter Dylan.

And on Thursday, the new mother, 29, took a moment out of her busy work day to pump breast milk for her three-month-old.

She posted a selfie while in sitting back in the hair and makeup chair during her photo shoot.
