Christina Aguilera is chic in black satin as she preps for concerts at historic Hollywood Bowl

She is a multi-platinum selling singer and a five-time Grammy Award winner.

And on Tuesday, pop icon Christina Aguilera thrilled her 7.5 million Instagram followers with images building buzz for her suite of concerts this month at Los Angeles, California’s historic outdoor venue the Hollywood Bowl.

The Genie in a Bottle songbird, 40, perched on a blue sofa in the first image, dressed in a fabulous and shiny black satin set featuring lapels and a plunging neckline.

On Tuesday: Christina Aguilera thrilled her Instagram followers by building buzz for her suite of concerts this month at LA’s historic outdoor venue the Hollywood Bowl

Xtina’s bright baby blues peeked out from over her rectangular sunglasses, which were fit for a rockstar.

The mother of two had her platinum blonde hair up in a high, long and sleek ponytail.

Aguilera had a microphone clipped to her lapel, as she surely visited the venue for a soundcheck ahead of her two concerts scheduled there in the middle of the month. 

She wore silver crucifix earrings and a matching pendant necklace. 

On stage: The Genie in a Bottle songbird was dressed in a fabulous and shiny black satin set featuring lapels and a plunging neckline

The Fighter singer’s makeup look was flawless, with a pale pink matte lip.

She sported transparent heels and rocked a bright neon manicure.

The rest of the images were styled as Polaroids, with the Beautiful hitmaker reclining on the sofa before posing for photos in the hallways and out on the stage of The Bowl itself. 

Resting beauty: The rest of the images were styled as Polaroids, with the Beautiful hitmaker reclining on the sofa in one

Christina will be performing in Hollywood on July 16 and 17 alongside the Los Angeles Philharmonic, featuring conductor Gustavo Dudamel.

Last week, Aguilera tweeted, “It’s an honor to collaborate with the incredibly talented @GustavoDudamel and the @LAPhil #HollywoodBowl July 16 & 17! Any guesses on the setlist?’

She concluded the tweet with the hashtag #TheBowlIsBack, referencing the return of live entertainment after the extended pandemic.
