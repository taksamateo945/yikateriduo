Anitta Brings Out Two Special Guests During Her Coachella Performance Pics!

Anitta brought the party to the 2022 Coachella Music Festival!

The 28-year-old Brazilian beauty hit the stage on Friday afternoon (April 15), kicking off the festivities for the music-filled weekend and brought two special guests on stage with her – Snoop Dogg AND Saweetie!

Anitta first brought out Snoop Dogg for her first song at the show – “Onda Diferente” – before welcoming Saweetie on stage for “Faking Love.”

Check out the videos from fans on Twitter!

“I’m going to bring a lot of Brazilian culture [to Coachella],” Anitta shared with SPIN magazine. “We’re going to dance a lot. We’re going to show how a Brazilian party is done. I think people are going to feel that there.”

She added, “I’m very excited for people to see it and get more familiar with my culture.”

If you missed it, stream Anitta‘s brand new album, Versions of Me, now!
