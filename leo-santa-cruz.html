Who is Leo Santa Cruz:

Leo Santa Cruz is a famous Boxer. He was born on August 10, 1988 and his birthplace is Huetamo, Mexico. Leo is also well known as, Bantamweight and featherweight boxer who has held the WBA super featherweight, WBC super bantamweight, and IBF bantamweight titles. In February 2017, he was ranked as the world’s 7th best pound-for-pound fighter in the world by BoxRec.

Leo is originated from Mexico. He won the WBA super featherweight title in August 2015 after defeating Abner Mares.
