Juancho Hernangomez Wife: Is the NBA Star Dating Anyone?

Juancho Hernangomez is a Spanish professional basketball player who currently plays for the Toronto Raptors of the NBA. He is also known for his co-starring role in the Netflix movie Hustle, alongside Adam Sandler and Queen Latifah. But what about his personal life? Does Juancho Hernangomez have a wife or a girlfriend? Here is everything we know about his love life.

Juancho Hernangomez Career: From Spain to NBA

Juancho Hernangomez was born and raised in Madrid, Spain. He comes from a basketball family, as his mother Margarita Geuer Draeger was an international basketball player who won a European championship with Spain in 1993. His father also played for Real Madrid and Estudiantes, and his older brother Willy is also an NBA player who currently plays for the New Orleans Pelicans.

Juancho started his professional career in 2012 with CB Estudiantes in Spain. He played with them until 2016, when he was selected by the Denver Nuggets with the 15th overall pick in the 2016 NBA draft. He signed his rookie contract with the Nuggets and made his NBA debut on October 29, 2016. He played for the Nuggets until 2020, when he was traded to the Minnesota Timberwolves. He spent one season with the Timberwolves before being traded again to the Boston Celtics in 2021. He was then waived by the Celtics and claimed by the San Antonio Spurs. He played only one game for the Spurs before being traded to the Utah Jazz, who then waived him as well. He was finally claimed by the Toronto Raptors on October 11, 2021.

Juancho has also represented Spain in international competitions. He won the gold medal at the 2019 FIBA Basketball World Cup and the silver medal at the 2022 EuroBasket. He also won two bronze medals at the European U-20 Championship and one bronze medal at the European U-18 Championship.

Juancho Hernangomez Movie: Hustle on Netflix

Juancho Hernangomez made his acting debut in 2022 with the Netflix movie Hustle, directed by Jeremiah Zagar and produced by Adam Sandler’s Happy Madison Productions. The movie follows a down-on-his-luck basketball scout (Sandler) who discovers a talented player (Hernangomez) overseas and brings him to America to prove that they both have what it takes to make it in the NBA.

Juancho plays the role of Bo Cruz, a charismatic and passionate basketball player from Spain who dreams of playing in the NBA. He impresses Sandler’s character with his skills and personality, but faces challenges and obstacles along the way. The movie also stars Queen Latifah, Robert Duvall, Ben Foster, Boban Marjanović, Anthony Edwards, and more.

Juancho revealed that he felt a connection to his character, as he had gone through similar experiences in his own career. He said that he had to overcome language barriers, cultural differences, and doubts from others to pursue his dreams of playing in the NBA. He also said that he enjoyed working with Adam Sandler and learned a lot from him as an actor and a producer.

Hustle was released on Netflix on November 19, 2022. The movie received positive reviews from critics and audiences alike, earning a 92% rating on Rotten Tomatoes. Juancho was praised for his performance and his chemistry with Sandler. Many fans were impressed by his acting skills and hoped to see him in more movies in the future.
