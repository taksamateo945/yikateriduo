About

Beetlejuice

“Beetlejuice” is a musical based on the 1988 film of the same name, with music and lyrics by Eddie Perfect and book by Scott Brown and Anthony King. The Broadway production opened at the Winter Garden Theatre in April 2019 and closed in March 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

The musical tells the story of a recently deceased couple, Barbara and Adam Maitland, who become ghosts haunting their former home. When the Deetz family moves in and begins renovating the house, the Maitlands try to scare them away with the help of a mischievous bio-exorcist named Beetlejuice.

The show features a mix of dark humor, catchy songs, and impressive visual effects, including puppetry and projection mapping. The lead roles, including Beetlejuice, Lydia Deetz, and Delia Deetz, are often played by comedic actors, and the show includes several fourth-wall-breaking moments.

While the Broadway run was relatively short-lived, “Beetlejuice” developed a dedicated fanbase and has since toured in the United States and internationally. The show received eight Tony Award nominations, including Best Musical, and won one award for Best Scenic Design.

FAQs

Why is Beetlejuice famous for?

Beetlejuice is famous for his role in the 1988 film of the same name.

He is a bio-exorcist who specializes in scaring away the living from haunted houses.

He is known for his flamboyant personality, his signature catchphrase “Beetlejuice,” and his willingness to do whatever it takes to scare people.

What did Beetlejuice do before he died?

Beetlejuice’s backstory is never fully explained in the film, but it is implied that he was a ghost who died in a freak accident.

Some fans have speculated that he may have been a serial killer or a con artist, while others believe that he was simply a misunderstood soul who was killed for no reason.

What makes Beetlejuice scary?

Beetlejuice is scary because he is unpredictable and unhinged.

He is not afraid to use violence or trickery to get what he wants, and he seems to take pleasure in scaring people.

His appearance is also somewhat disturbing, with his pale skin, sharp teeth, and wild hair.

What’s the story of Beetlejuice?

The story of Beetlejuice follows the Maitlands, a recently deceased couple who are trying to haunt their old house.

They enlist the help of Beetlejuice to scare away the new owners, but Beetlejuice’s antics soon get out of hand.

The Maitlands must find a way to stop Beetlejuice before he destroys their lives.
