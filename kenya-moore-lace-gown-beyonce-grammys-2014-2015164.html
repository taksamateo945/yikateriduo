Kenya Moore Wears Same Lace Gown as Beyonce from the Grammys 2014

It’s a classic diva showdown! 

Kenya Moore once said she often gets mistaken for Beyonce, and it seems the outspoken Real Housewives of Atlanta cast member is doing all she can to keep up the tale.

For the show’s three-part reunion episode, beginning on Sunday, April 26, at 8 p.m. on Bravo, the former pageant queen wore a look straight from her supposed doppelganger’s closet. 

“You ready for this? Starts in 10 days… #RHOA,” Andy Cohen captioned a picture with Moore, Claudia Jordan, Kandi Burruss, Cynthia Bailey, NeNe Leakes, Phaedra Parks, and Porsha Williams, all dressed in all white via Instagram.
