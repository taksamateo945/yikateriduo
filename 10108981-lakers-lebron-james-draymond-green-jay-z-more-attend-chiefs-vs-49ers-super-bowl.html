Lakers' LeBron James, Draymond Green, Jay-Z, More Attend Chiefs vs. 49ers Super Bowl | News, Scores,

Andy Lyons/Getty Images

The stars are out for Super Bowl LVIII.

While Taylor Swift has generated many of the headlines when it comes to celebrities at Kansas City Chiefs' games, she is far from the only one at Allegiant Stadium for Sunday's showdown between the AFC champions and the San Francisco 49ers.

Jay-Z, LeBron James, Shaquille O'Neal, Paul Rudd, Draymond Green, Ice Spice and, of course, Swift were in attendance before kickoff:

The question now is whether all those athletes and celebrities will see Patrick Mahomes add to his legacy as one of the best quarterbacks in the history of the NFL. The Chiefs are playing in their fourth Super Bowl in five years and have the chance to lift the Lombardi Trophy for the third time in that span.

San Francisco is loaded with star power in its own right, though, as Christian McCaffrey, Brandon Aiyuk, Nick Bosa, Fred Warner, Deebo Samuel, George Kittle and more will do their best to counter the Mahomes and Travis Kelce combination.

Whoever ends up winning the Super Bowl will do so in front of some of America's biggest stars.
