Matthew Macfadyen Age, Biography

Matthew Macfadyen was born on October 17, 1974, in Great Yarmouth, Norfolk, England, United Kingdom. Meinir (née Owen), a drama teacher and former actress, and Martin Macfadyen, an oil engineer, are his parents. His maternal grandparents were Welsh, while his paternal grandparents were Scottish. He is of British nationality and of mixed ethnicity.

Jamie Macfadyen, his younger brother, is his sibling. As a result of his father’s occupation, he was raised in a variety of locations, including Jakarta, Indonesia. He attended schools in England (including Louth, Lincolnshire), Scotland, and Indonesia before being accepted to the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art at the age of 17.

Matthew Macfadyen’s Wife

Matthew Macfadyen is a married man with a beautiful wife named Keeley Hawes. In 2002, he began dating actress and producer Keeley Hawes, with whom he co-starred in the spy drama series Spooks.
