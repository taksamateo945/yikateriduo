Meryl Streeps Daughters Appear in New Ad Campaign See the Stunning Photos!

Meryl Streep‘s kids are all grown up!

The actress’ three daughters, Mamie, 31, Grace, 28, and Louisa Gummer, 23, appear in a new ads for H&M’s sister brand, & Other Stories — and they look just like their famous mom!

Louisa, Grace, Meryl and Mamie in June 2004.

The celebrity scion — who work as models and actresses — showcase the brand’s newest collection of shoes, bags and accessories, which will be available for online purchase starting March 5, 2015.

“I was thrilled when &OS enlisted my friends and favorite sisters, Mamie, Grace and Louisa Gummer, to star in the campaign, which was shot by legendary photographer Stephen Shore,” the designer of the new pieces said on her website.

From left to right: Grace, Louisa and Mamie Gummer.

The sisters’ father is sculptor Don Gummer, 68, whom Meryl, 65, married in September 1978. The couple are also parents to a son, Henry Wolfe Gummer, 35.
