Little Mix reveal naughty song 'A.D.I.D.A.S'

When Little Mix won the X-Factor, they were a lovely quartet of cutesy teens with a love for dayglo eyeshadow and colorful clothes. This time is definitely over for our four favourite girls.

“Perrie keeps forcing me to say the lyrics because she likes to see me squirm; look at Twitter, i don’t want to say them out loud” Said Jade blushing when she was asked to recite the lyrics of their new song A.D.I.D.A.S which basically stands for ‘All Day I Dream About Sex’. 

Chatting to the mirror, Perrie Edwards said “A.D.I.D.A.S is the most shocking one i think. But we don’t actually say ‘sex’ it’s a ‘shush’ now. It’s naughty but innocent.” She added “Literally when my dad heard it he was like ‘no, girl, turn it off.”

But Jesy Nelson laughed at the idea that the song was innocent, leading to some of the graphic lyrics to be revealed by the girls themselves.

“It sounds so wrong saying it” said Thirlwall before revealing she sings “Slaving in the kitchen, iced you a cake then I served you a plate, but that ain’t what you ate.” NAUGHTY. 

Talking about the raunchy song on their new album ‘Get Weird’, Jade explained “It’s like, you know when you first meet someone in that beginning stage and then you first start getting a bit naughty naughty and you wanna do it all the time?” 

The girls also added “We’re adult now, we’ve had relationships and we can be naughty”. We haven’t had our earhole near the track yet, but we can already say that we are ready to hear it here at @CelebMix !
